When should you reboot the track?

  • SRM fails to respond to its own reboot or recovery protocols
  • Instruments lose communication with the line
  • IOM-1 or IOM-2 need multiple recoveries within a short time frame
  • Samples fail to go the instruments, storage. or lane 19 and thus "circle the track"

  1. Srm offline initialize conveyor to move remaining racks to storage
  2. Make sure centrifuges are empty. Wait until they are placed on track
  3. Remove lanes 1-4 on IOM 1 and minimize the IUI
  4. Open DREAM log in
  5. Select system then purge allow all remaining samples to return to lane 19 to track how many in DREAM click counters then track status when carriers on main track-full = 0, pause track
  6. Unlock all racks from IOM-1
  7.
